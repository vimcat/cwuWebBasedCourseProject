Success With Web Based Courses

Distance learning made easier

If you are having trouble with your online class software or you don't know where to begin, we're here to help!

We are using the free Mikogo screen sharing software to help you.

Canvas

Your online classes will be in Canvas. CWU Canvas help page has detailed information on Canvas. The Canvas Support Line is also really good. They are available 24/7 to help you. Toll Free Support Line: (877) 399-8897

DreamSpark

CWU participates in the Microsoft DreamSpark program. Check your CWU email for account information to access the FREE software that you may need to use during your classes. If you do not have your DreamSpark account information email, contact your counselor. For instructions on how to use DreamSpark, download the PDF.
